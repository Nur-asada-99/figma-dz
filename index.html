Relay makes it easy to understand precisely what you’re earning, spending and saving so you can make
the smartest decisions for your business.

1

Get the income
overview

Collect income in one place to see what

payments—including ACH transfers, wires,
checks and payouts from tools like PayPal,
Stripe, Square and more.

2

Earmark the
essentials

Use up to 20 checking accounts to organize
and allocate income for things like day-to-
day expenses and payroll. Automate the
transfer of income into each account using
dollar amounts or percentages.

You can also use checking accounts to set
up, separate and continually contribute to
cash reserves for taxes, investments in your
growth and unexpected emergencies.
